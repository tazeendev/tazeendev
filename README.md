# About Me

I am **Tazeen Zahra**, a Computer Science student from **Pakistan 🇵🇰** with a strong passion for **Artificial Intelligence, Computer Vision, Robotics, and Autonomous Systems**.

I enjoy building intelligent perception systems that enable machines to understand and interact with the world. My work focuses on applying modern AI techniques to solve real-world challenges through research, experimentation, and practical implementation.

My current interests include:

- Bird's Eye View (BEV) Perception
- Computer Vision
- Object Detection & Tracking
- Semantic Segmentation
- Vision Transformers
- Large Vision Models
- Robotics
- Reinforcement Learning
- Multi-modal AI
- Autonomous Driving

I actively build AI projects using **PyTorch**, **OpenCV**, and modern deep learning frameworks while continuously studying the latest research papers from **CVPR**, **ICCV**, **ECCV**, **NeurIPS**, and **ICRA**.

Alongside coding, I document my learning through technical writing on **Medium**, where I share tutorials, research paper summaries, implementation guides, and insights from my AI journey.

> **Open to AI Research Internships, research collaborations, and opportunities in Computer Vision, Robotics, and Autonomous Systems.**
